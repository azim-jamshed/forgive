Editor's Choice Designation

Selection for the prestigious Editor's Choice program of iUniverse means that an author's manuscript has undergone an editorial review and received high marks in numeric scoring and feedback that address:

  • Theme
  • Character development and depth
  • Plot and story line resolution
  • Pace and setting of story
  • Effective use of dialogue
  • Editorial and stylistic precision
  • Marketability and readability
