Send Warmth to Romania

In March of 2005,Crestview Baptist Church responded to an invitation from Dr. Paul Negrut of Emanuel University (http://www.emanuel.ro/) by sending a team of 38 members to Oradea, Romania. The purpose of the trip was three-fold: * Provide a music and evangelism program of encouragement to the university and regional Baptist churches. * Have fellowship with the young ministers, teachers and businessmen and women in training at the university. * Assess and begin planning for future mission and ministry opportunities. While in the country, the team provided music and preaching at several churches. They took part in street evangelism, visited orphanages and took part in many social service activities provided by the school. Over the course of the trip there were several professions of faith made during ministry activities. Some members met with students, provided teaching and lectures and met with local business leaders. Over 200 Romanian bibles were provided by the group and, later, a scholarship donation of over $1000 was given to the seminary.

The Piecemakers, a needlework group that meets at Crestview Baptist Church, will be knitting and crocheting baby blankets and children's sweaterse to send with the 2007 Romania team.
